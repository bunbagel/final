Fox Dance Tutorial

In the island of foxes in Japan,
there are the sacred foxes of the land.
Little is known to how they got there,
but there is a dance known to celebrate them and their blessings.

Click the play button below to start!



First we start with a bow
As most introductions start with.

Next we clap twice on our left and right.

Then we get low to the earth.

After that we bring our energy up to the sky.

Finally we end with a stomp from each leg
to signify our end.

Of course you can add your own flare to it as well.



May their blessings be with you.
